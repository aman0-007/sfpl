Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide a quote?

We typically provide detailed quotes within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent requests, we can provide preliminary quotes within 30 minutes.

Do you handle dangerous goods?

Yes, we are certified to handle dangerous goods shipments by air and sea. Our team is trained in IATA and IMDG regulations for safe transportation.

Can you provide door-to-door service?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive door-to-door services including pickup, transportation, customs clearance, and final delivery to your destination.

What tracking options do you provide?

We provide real-time tracking through our online portal, email updates, and SMS notifications. You'll always know where your shipment is.
